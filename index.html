Profile

I am a multidisciplinary designer operating at the intersection of industrial form, digital interfaces, and visual storytelling.

My work centers on the visual ecosystem of heavy machinery. This encompasses the entire pipeline: from building complex 3D scenes and textures for marketing renders, to designing the UI components drivers interact with daily, down to the precise technical graphics on the machine itself.

I believe that a premium product requires consistency across every touchpoint—whether it’s a photorealistic render, a digital icon, or a physical safety label.
